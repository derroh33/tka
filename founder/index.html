WINFRED MBESA MALONZA

Founder & President, Tour Kenya Adventures

Welcome to Tour Kenya Adventures! I’m Winfred Mbesa Malonza ( popularly known as Winnie ), the proud founder and president of this vibrant tour operating company based in the heart of Nairobi, Kenya. My passion for travel and adventure ignited this journey, and it continues to inspire every experience we craft for our travellers.

Growing up surrounded by Kenya’s breathtaking landscapes and diverse cultures, I developed an insatiable love for exploring the world around me. From the verdant hills of the Great Rift Valley to the sandy shores of the Indian Ocean, I’ve traversed numerous landscapes, each adventure deepening my appreciation for the richness of our planet.

At Tour Kenya Adventures, my team and I believe that travel is not just about reaching a destination but about creating meaningful connections and unforgettable memories. We curate exceptional experiences that immerse our guests in the beauty and culture of Kenya and beyond. Every expedition is thoughtfully designed to showcase the essence of our stunning locales, whether you’re embarking on a thrilling safari, savouring local cuisine, or engaging with the vibrant traditions of our communities.

I invite you to join us on this incredible journey, where adventure awaits at every turn. Together, let’s explore the wonders of our world and create stories that will last a lifetime.
